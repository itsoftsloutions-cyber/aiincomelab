## Outreach Best Practices

1. Personalize every email — reference something specific from their blog
2. Keep it under 150 words
3. Follow up once after 7 days if no response
4. Never use automated outreach tools (they burn relationships)
5. Track all outreach in a spreadsheet: URL, contact, date, status
6. Offer genuine value before asking for a link
7. Build relationships, not just backlinks
