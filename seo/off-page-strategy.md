# Off-Page SEO & Backlink Strategy — AIIncomeLab

## Current State
- 13 published posts on AI tools, productivity, and online income
- GitHub Pages hosting (github.io subdomain)
- One Twitter/X social profile linked
- No structured backlink acquisition in place
- AdSense active, GA4 placeholder

## Priority Backlink Sources (by effort vs. impact)

### Tier 1 — High Impact, Low Effort (Do First)
- [ ] Social profile creation & unification (LinkedIn, Facebook, Pinterest, Reddit, Quora, Medium, Dev.to)
- [ ] Business listing directories (Google Business Profile, Bing Places, Yelp)
- [ ] Blog/startup directories (BetaList, Product Hunt, SaaSHub, AlternativeTo)
- [ ] HARO / Connectively reporter requests (free backlinks from journalists)
- [ ] GitHub README/profile link (repo descriptions, profile README)

### Tier 2 — Medium Impact, Medium Effort
- [ ] Guest posting on relevant AI/tech/income blogs (see outreach templates)
- [ ] Broken link building on relevant resource pages
- [ ] Resource page link insertion (curated lists of AI tools, blogs)
- [ ] Quora / Reddit contextual links (valuable answers linking to relevant posts)
- [ ] Medium cross-publishing (republish excerpts with canonical back to original)
- [ ] Dev.to cross-publishing (tech audience, canonical support)

### Tier 3 — Lower Impact, Ongoing Effort
- [ ] Forum profile links (relevant niche forums)
- [ ] Web 2.0 properties (WordPress.com, Wix, Weebly — thin but diversify link profile)
- [ ] Social bookmarking (Reddit, StumbleUpon/Digg equivalents)
- [ ] Commenting on relevant blogs (adds value + link)
- [ ] Podcast appearances / interviews

## Quarterly Backlink Targets
- Month 1-3: 10-15 diverse backlinks from Tier 1 sources
- Month 4-6: 20-30 backlinks including 3-5 guest posts from Tier 2
- Month 7-12: Maintain 5-10 new backlinks/month, focus on authority

## Content Formats That Attract Backlinks (Linkable Assets)
1. **Original research / surveys** — e.g., "We surveyed 100 AI users: here's what they earn"
2. **Ultimate guides** — Comprehensive 5000+ word tutorials
3. **Tool comparisons** — Side-by-side feature/price matrices (link magnets)
4. **Infographics** — Visual summaries of AI income data
5. **Expert roundups** — Quotes from industry professionals
6. **Free tools / calculators** — ROI calculator, income potential estimator
7. **Case studies** — Real AI income experiments with documented results
