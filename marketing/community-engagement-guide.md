# AIIncomeLab - Community Engagement Guide

## Goal
Drive referral traffic, build topical authority, and establish AIIncomeLab as a trusted voice in AI/make-money communities. No spam. 80% value-add, 20% link sharing.

---

## Reddit

### Target Subreddits
| Subreddit | Focus | Weekly Effort |
|-----------|-------|---------------|
| r/Entrepreneur | Business-building, side projects | 3 comments/week |
| r/AI | AI discussions, tools | 2 comments/week |
| r/makemoneyonline | Income methods | 2 comments/week |
| r/sidehustle | Side income | 2 comments/week |
| r/Blogging | Content creation | 1 comment/week |

### Strategy Per Subreddit

**r/Entrepreneur**
- Share real experiences with AI tools in business
- Comment on "what tool saved you the most time" threads
- Post only when you have a unique angle backed by data

**r/AI**
- Focus on practical tool comparisons
- Comment on "which AI tool is overrated" discussions
- Avoid self-promotion entirely for first 60 days

**r/makemoneyonline**
- Share realistic income numbers (from blog transparently)
- Answer "is AI blogging still worth it" questions
- Link to blog only when it directly answers the question

**r/sidehustle**
- Post beginner-friendly AI tool recommendations
- Engage on "I need $500/month" threads

**r/Blogging**
- Share SEO/Content strategies that worked
- Traffic milestones from the blog

### Engagement Rules
1. No link in first comment. Make it conversational.
2. If relevant, offer to share a resource = natural link opportunity.
3. Use UTM: `?utm_source=reddit&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=r_subredditname`
4. Never post affiliate links.
5. Build karma before posting in restricted subs.

## Quora

### Target Topics
- Artificial Intelligence
- Making Money Online
- Blogging
- Side Hustles
- Productivity

### Strategy
- Answer 3-5 questions/week
- Format: Detailed walkthrough (200-500 words)
- Open with direct answer to the question
- Include personal experience ("I run AIIncomeLab and...")
- Soft-link to blog posts that expand on the answer
- Use UTM: `?utm_source=quora&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=topicname`

### Question Templates to Search
- "What is the best AI tool for [purpose]?"
- "How can I make money with AI in 2026?"
- "Is [tool] worth the money?"
- "How do beginners start making money online?"
