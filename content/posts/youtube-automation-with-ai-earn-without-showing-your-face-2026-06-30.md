## Pick a profitable niche

Finance, health, personal development, and tech education niches consistently pay the highest YouTube CPM rates, often $10-$30 per thousand views compared to $1-$5 for general entertainment.

Avoid oversaturated niches like motivation quotes, video game compilations, or generic movie commentary where thousands of faceless channels are already competing for the same audience.

Check whether existing faceless channels in your target niche are already monetized by looking for ads on their videos before committing your time to content creation.
