## Frequently asked questions

**Is Perplexity better than ChatGPT for research?**

Yes, for fact-based research because it cites sources in real time. ChatGPT is better for creative writing and drafting where source citation matters less.

**Can I trust Perplexity's sources?**

The sources are real links you can verify, but the AI's summary of those sources may contain errors. Always click through and verify critical facts from the original source.

**Is the free tier sufficient for content research?**

Yes for casual use. The free tier covers several hundred queries per day. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited queries and additional features like image generation.
