## AI agency services (lowest barrier to entry)

Sell AI content, SEO, or social media services to local businesses lacking time or skills to execute themselves.

Startup cost is zero since AI free tiers cover basic client deliverables until you have revenue.

## AI-powered SaaS (highest ceiling)

Build a tool wrapping AI APIs with a focused use case for a defined audience without competing with general platforms.

An MVP is achievable in weeks using no-code tools like Bubble with GPT-4 or Claude API access.

Target underserved niches like real estate agents or fitness coaches where existing tools miss specific workflows.

## AI content and media businesses

Niche blogs, YouTube channels, and newsletters build an audience to monetize through advertisers and affiliates.

Fastest path to ,000/month is 12-18 months of consistent publishing in a niche with proven ad demand.

The audience is the asset ? advertisers pay for access as long as content stays relevant.

## AI training and education

Courses and workshops teaching businesses how to adopt AI represent a massive and growing market.

Most businesses are still early in AI adoption, creating enormous demand for practical education.

Your first workshop can go from idea to published in 2 weeks using AI for curriculum and marketing.

## Evaluating which to start

Match to your skills: agency for client management experience, SaaS for product building, content for writing or video.

Pick one and commit ? starting three AI businesses simultaneously means executing none well.

Revenue within 90 days is achievable for agency and education, while SaaS and content take 6-18 months.
